Information about Joey Empire

  • History: The Joey Empire's origins can be traced to somewhere between January and March 17th of 2023. Joey Nelson was Minister of Defence of Hesperia and is a veteran of the Lecinist Uprising. Under suspicions of conspiracy against Hesperia, The RCVA was to spy on Nelson. This led to Nelson rebelling and declaring Joey Empire as sovereign.
  • Leadership: The only two known residents of the Joey Empire are Joey Nelson himself as Emperor and Iusef, whose role is unknown... It is also undecided if he is in the Joey Empire or not.
  • Philosophy: According to Joey Nelson, the religion of Joey Empire is "Mercantilism". I'm not sure what this means but yeah
  • Territories:The territories of the Joey Empire are St. Pierre et Miquelon, Paris Texas, London Ontario, and Hell Michigan, and Pyongyang
